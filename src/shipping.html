Shipping

USA Shipping

Shipping comes at no cost to you with a minimum purchase of $75 or more (before tax and promotional discounts). This offer is valid on standard U.S. ground shipping to delivery addresses within the 48 continental states (excluding Hawaii and Alaska).

Orders are usually processed within 1-2 business days (weekends and holidays excluded). For expedited and express shipping methods, additional charges apply. Once your order is shipped, you will receive a shipment email entailing shipment and tracking details.

Please note: Pre-orders are unavailable to ship until 10-14 business days of order. Expedited and express shipping methods do not affect the delivery time or pre-order items.

If you have not received your package and the tracking information states that it was delivered, you must address this with the selected carrier. PoshShop is not responsible for packages that are stated as delivered.

Express Shipping

Express orders placed Monday through Friday before 12:00PM PST will ship out the same day and orders placed after 12:00PM PST will be shipped out the next business day (weekends and holidays excluded). Orders will usually arrive within 2-3 business days from the shipped date.

Next Day Shipping

Next Day Shipping is available on orders placed before 12:00PM PST Monday through Friday. Orders usually arrive the following business day or by the end of the day (excluding holidays and weekends). Please note that Saturday delivery is not available at this time. Next Day Shipping is only available to U.S. addresses in the 48 contiguous states. Alaska, Hawaii, international delivery addresses are excluded.

International Shipping

Fabfoxx offers shipping to a variety of international countries (listed below). Orders are usually processed within 1-2 business days. Depending on location, both flat rate and expedited shipping is available upon checkout. Once your order is shipped, you will receive a shipment email entailing shipment and tracking details.

Please note: Pre-orders are unavailable to ship until 10-14 business days of order. Expedited and express shipping methods do not affect the delivery time or pre-order items.

Tax & Duties

All applicable customs, import duties and fees, taxes, and any other charges are the sole responsibility of the customer. Depending on the shipping option selected at checkout, you may have the option to prepay duties and taxes on your order. If you choose to prepay, the price of duties and taxes is 100% guaranteed! No further fees will be due upon delivery. Please note: Posh Shop has no control over your package once it leaves the USA. For more information regarding your country's custom policies please contact your local customs office.
